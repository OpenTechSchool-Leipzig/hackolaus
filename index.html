Adaption Leipzig IGB e.V.

Abstinence is not everything, but without abstinence everything is nothing. According to the mission statement of the Institut fÃ¼r Gesundheit und Bildung e.V., it is our task to encourage and enable addicted people to lead an abstinent life. An abstinent life is seen as the best possibility for addicts to lead a satisfied, independent and self-determined life. Adaptation treatment is part of the services for medical rehabilitation of addicts and represents phase II of withdrawal treatment. Adaptation is also to be understood as an opportunity to review and possibly modify one's therapy experiences in everyday life. During the treatment period, patients should be supported in building up favorable conditions for an abstinent life. In addition to the therapeutic offers, comprehensive support in all practical life and social work issues is part of our work focus. The main topics are: testing the ability to abstain under real conditions, clarification of the professional situation, development of social skills, development of future perspectives, life planning, organization of free time, organization of social relationships, management of money. ... read more

CABL e.V. - Treat Equally

We have regular consultation hours for people without health insurance since November 2019, where we provide advice on access to the health care system and issue so-called anonymous treatment vouchers if necessary, when access to regular health care cannot be created. No identification is made; we follow the principle that every person is entitled to medical care. All those involved in arranging, implementing and paying for your medical care will only use a pseudonym for you that cannot be traced back to you. ... read more

Code for Afrika e.V. Leipzig

We are a non-profit organization that promotes tech education and entrepreneurship among African diaspora in Germany and youth in Africa. We offer people the opportunity to build capacity that will help them to create new ventures. For this purpose, we introduce kids and adults to different technologies using novel platforms. We organize workshops on innovative business modelling for building new and sustainable ventures and we offer training courses on how to teach coding for trainers in collaboration with our partners. Nightline Leipzig

The Nightline Leipzig is a helpline with late office hours. It is an offer for students by students. We are all volunteers and offer our service five days a week during the semester. Whether lovesickness, panic attacks or dropping out of studies, we have a sympathetic ear for everyone.
