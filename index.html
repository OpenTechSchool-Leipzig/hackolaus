Hack for good

Event details

When the nights get longer and the weather outside is frightful, we need some warmth in our hearts.

Many people sacrifice their time and energy to create this warmth by giving and contributing to the benefit of the weak ones or the ones that can’t defend themselves.

Many NGOs, associations and individuals work hard to give to social, environmental or other sustainability causes. We believe that they should not only give but also receive, that society should give back to them. And we believe that making can make a difference to their mission, especially in the darker days.

This is the idea behind the Hackolaus hackathon that premieres this year.

FAQ

Is there a Code of Conduct?
Yes, you can find it HERE

Will the venue be open overnight?
No, the venue will close at ___ .

I can’t stay the whole time, can I still participate?
Of course! We realize that a weekend is a long time and people may need to attend to other commitments. We do, however, strongly encourage you to be present on Friday to see the NGOs pitch ideas and find a team.

What if I don't know how to code?
You are still welcome! We have several challenges that don’t require any coding skills. As long as you come willing to learn and contribute ideas.

What do I bring?
Bring your laptop, mobile phone, scratch paper and curiousity. We will provide food, drinks, wi-fi, power and some christmas treats.
