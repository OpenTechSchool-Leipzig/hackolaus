Coming soon, in Autumn 2023: hack for good #3

hackathon to give back to the good

Oct 6th - 8th 2023, Leipzig

Many NGOs, associations and individuals work hard to give to social, environmental or other sustainability causes. We believe that they should not only give but also receive, that society should give back to them. This is why we are collaborating on a hackathon to support these organizations with solutions to help them focus more on delivering impact.
